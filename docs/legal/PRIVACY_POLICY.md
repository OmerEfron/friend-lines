# Privacy Policy

**Friendlines**  
**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Introduction

Friendlines ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Name
- Username
- Email address
- Password (stored securely as a hash)
- Profile avatar (optional)

### User-Generated Content
When you use Friendlines, you may create:
- Newsflashes (text and images)
- Group memberships
- Friend connections
- Bookmarked content

### Device Information
To provide push notifications and improve service reliability, we collect:
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Push notification token (Expo Push Token)
- Device identifier (derived from device characteristics)

### Usage Data
We may collect information about how you interact with the app, including:
- Features you use
- Time spent in the app
- Crash reports and error logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Create and manage your account
- Enable social features (friendships, groups, newsflashes)
- Send push notifications about activity relevant to you
- Improve and maintain the app
- Respond to your requests and support inquiries
- Ensure the security and integrity of our services

## Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:
- **With other users:** Your profile information (name, username, avatar) and newsflashes are visible to your friends and group members.
- **Service providers:** We use third-party services (e.g., cloud hosting, push notification services) that process data on our behalf.
- **Legal requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption.
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
- We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission.
- Data is stored on AWS infrastructure in secure data centers.

## Third-Party Services

Friendlines uses the following third-party services:
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS):** Cloud hosting and data storage
- **Expo:** Push notification delivery
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM):** Android push notifications
- **Apple Push Notification Service (APNs):** iOS push notifications

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Update or correct your account information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt out of push notifications (via device settings)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

## Children's Privacy

Friendlines is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of Friendlines after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:
